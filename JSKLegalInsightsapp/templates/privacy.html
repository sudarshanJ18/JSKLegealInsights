Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 18, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to JSK Legal Insights. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our website.

2. Information We Collect

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used for:

  • Providing access to legal articles, case law summaries, and AI-driven tools.

4. Data Sharing & Security

We do not sell your personal information. Your data is securely stored and only shared with third-party services necessary for website operation (e.g., analytics, hosting providers).

5. Your Rights

  • You can request access to your personal data.
  • You may request deletion of your account and data.
  • You can opt out of non-essential data collection.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at our contact page.
